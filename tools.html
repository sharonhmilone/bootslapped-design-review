ActiveCampaign

Email Marketing · CRM · Automation

When Kit stops being enough, ActiveCampaign is usually the next call. It's not a beginner tool — it assumes you already know what a funnel is and need to build one properly.

The CRM integration is what separates it from most email platforms. If you're running any kind of sales process alongside your list — even informally — the ability to tag contacts, track pipeline stage, and trigger sequences off sales activity is where this earns its cost. The pricing is not friendly to tiny lists, which is why this is not the first recommendation. But if you're managing a list past 5k subscribers with real commercial intent and need automations more complex than a single sequence, it's the right move.
